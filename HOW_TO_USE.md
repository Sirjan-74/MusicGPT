# How to Use MusicGPT - Step by Step Guide

## 🎯 Quick Start

1. **Open the Application**: Launch the MusicGPT web app in your browser
2. **Describe Your Music**: Type what you want in the text area
3. **Click Generate**: Press the "Generate Music" button
4. **Listen & Download**: Use the audio player to listen and download

## 📝 Detailed Instructions

### Step 1: Describe Your Music
In the text area labeled "Describe your music", write a detailed description of the music you want to create.

**Examples of good descriptions:**
- "uplifting electronic music with synth melodies and driving beat"
- "epic orchestral soundtrack for a fantasy adventure"
- "chill lofi beats for studying and relaxation"
- "energetic synthwave with retro 80s vibes"
- "melancholic piano piece with rain sounds"

**Tips:**
- Be specific about instruments
- Mention the mood or emotion
- Include genre references
- Describe the tempo or energy level

### Step 2: Choose Genre
Click on one of the genre buttons to select your music style:
- **Electronic**: Synths, beats, electronic sounds
- **Pop**: Catchy melodies, mainstream style
- **Rock**: Guitars, drums, energetic
- **Classical**: Orchestral, traditional
- **Jazz**: Swing, improvisation, brass
- **Hip-Hop**: Beats, rap-style rhythms
- **Ambient**: Atmospheric, background
- **LoFi**: Chill, relaxed, study music
- **Synthwave**: 80s retro, electronic
- **Orchestral**: Epic, cinematic, strings
- **Folk**: Acoustic, traditional
- **Blues**: Soulful, guitar-driven

### Step 3: Set the Mood
Select the emotional tone of your music:
- **Uplifting**: Positive, inspiring, happy
- **Melancholic**: Sad, reflective, emotional
- **Energetic**: Fast, exciting, dynamic
- **Calm**: Peaceful, relaxing, gentle
- **Dark**: Mysterious, intense, dramatic
- **Romantic**: Loving, passionate, tender
- **Epic**: Grand, cinematic, powerful
- **Mysterious**: Intriguing, suspenseful
- **Happy**: Joyful, cheerful, bright
- **Sad**: Emotional, sorrowful, deep
- **Focused**: Concentrated, determined

### Step 4: Adjust Duration
Use the slider to set how long your music should be:
- **5-10 seconds**: Quick previews, ringtones
- **10-20 seconds**: Social media clips, samples
- **20-30 seconds**: Full musical ideas, demos

### Step 5: Set Tempo (BPM)
Adjust the beats per minute:
- **60-80 BPM**: Slow, relaxed
- **80-120 BPM**: Medium, walking pace
- **120-160 BPM**: Fast, energetic
- **160-200 BPM**: Very fast, intense

### Step 6: Generate Music
Click the **"Generate Music"** button with the sparkles icon ✨

**What happens:**
1. The button shows "Generating Music..." with a spinner
2. The system simulates AI processing (2 seconds)
3. Your music appears in the "Generated Music" section
4. The audio player automatically loads

### Step 7: Listen to Your Music
Use the audio player controls:
- **Play/Pause**: Start and stop playback
- **Volume**: Adjust sound level
- **Progress Bar**: Skip to different parts
- **Time Display**: See current position

### Step 8: Download Your Music
Click the **"Download"** button to save your music as an MP3 file.

The file will be named: `musicgpt-[timestamp].mp3`

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### Writing Better Prompts
1. **Combine elements**: "electronic music with piano melodies and ambient pads"
2. **Be specific**: "fast tempo rock with distorted guitars and heavy drums"
3. **Use adjectives**: "dreamy, atmospheric synthwave with arpeggiated melodies"
4. **Reference artists**: "in the style of Hans Zimmer with epic brass sections"

### Parameter Combinations
- **Study Music**: LoFi genre + Calm mood + 70 BPM
- **Workout Music**: Electronic genre + Energetic mood + 140 BPM
- **Sleep Music**: Ambient genre + Calm mood + 60 BPM
- **Gaming Music**: Orchestral genre + Epic mood + 120 BPM

### Advanced Techniques
1. **Layer descriptions**: Start with genre, add instruments, then mood
2. **Use contrasts**: "calm melody with energetic percussion"
3. **Specify structure**: "build up slowly with a big drop at 15 seconds"
4. **Reference scenes**: "music for a space exploration documentary"

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**No sound playing:**
- Check your device volume
- Ensure audio isn't muted in browser
- Try refreshing the page

**Download not working:**
- Check browser permissions
- Ensure pop-ups aren't blocked
- Try a different browser

**Generation taking too long:**
- Normal simulation time is 2 seconds
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh if stuck

**Interface looks wrong:**
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

## 🎓 Learning Resources

### Music Theory Basics
- **Tempo**: Speed of the music (BPM = Beats Per Minute)
- **Genre**: Style or category of music
- **Mood**: Emotional feeling of the music
- **Duration**: Length of the music piece

### AI Music Concepts
- **Transformer Model**: AI architecture used for generation
- **Tokenization**: How music is represented digitally
- **Conditioning**: How we control the AI's output
- **Sampling Rate**: Audio quality measurement
